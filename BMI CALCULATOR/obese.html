Obesity Management Tips: Do's & Don'ts

Do's:

  • Increase fiber intake with whole grains, vegetables, and fruits.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking enough water daily.
  • Engage in at least 30-45 minutes of physical activity daily.
  • Maintain a healthy and balanced diet with controlled portions.
  • Get adequate sleep to regulate hunger hormones.

Don'ts:

  • Avoid excessive sugar, processed foods, and trans fats.
  • Do not skip meals, as it may lead to overeating later.
  • Limit consumption of sugary beverages like soda and packed juices.
  • Avoid a sedentary lifestyle; try to stay active throughout the day.
