Exercises for Weight Gain :

Barbell Squat

Squats target the lower body, especially the quads, hamstrings, and glutes. Adding weight (barbell) enhances muscle growth, helping you gain lean muscle mass.

Deadlifts

Deadlifts engage the entire body, focusing on the back, glutes, and legs. It is an excellent exercise for increasing overall muscle mass and strength.


Some More Exercises:

  • Bench Press
  • Pull-Ups/Chin-Ups
  • Lunges
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press
  • Rows (Barbell/Dumbbell Rows)
  • Leg Press
  • Dips

Weight Gain Tips:What to Do & What Not to Do

What You Should Do:

  • Eat more high-calorie foods like nuts, seeds, dairy, and lean meats.
  • Have 5-6 small meals a day.
  • Drink smoothies and shakes to increase your calorie intake.
  • Focus on strength training exercises to build muscle.
  • Get enough sleep for muscle recovery.

What You Should Avoid:

  • Skip meals or go for long periods without eating.
  • Overeat unhealthy junk food with little nutritional value.
  • Engage in excessive cardio exercises.
  • Ignore proper hydration.
  • Neglect your mental health; stress can affect your appetite and weight gain.
