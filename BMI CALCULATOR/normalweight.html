Normal Weight Diet Plan & Tips

Balanced Diet Plan

Whole Grain Bread

Rich in fiber and nutrients, whole grain bread helps maintain energy levels and keeps digestion healthy. Best enjoyed with nut butter or eggs.

Yogurt

Packed with protein and probiotics, Greek yogurt is excellent for digestion and muscle maintenance. Add fruits or honey for extra taste!

Fresh Vegetables

Loaded with essential vitamins and minerals, fresh vegetables help maintain a well-balanced diet and overall well-being.

Healthy Lifestyle Tips: What to Do & What Not to Do

What You Should Do:

  • Eat a well-balanced diet with proteins, carbs, and fats.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Include fresh fruits and vegetables in your meals.
  • Exercise regularly to maintain fitness.
  • Ensure proper sleep and rest.

What You Should Avoid:

  • Skipping meals or having an irregular diet.
  • Eating excessive processed or junk food.
  • Overeating or undereating.
  • Ignoring physical activity.
  • Not maintaining proper sleep patterns.
