Weight Loss Tips: What to Do & What Not to Do

What You Should Do:

  • Eat more fiber-rich foods like vegetables, fruits, and whole grains.
  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes daily, focusing on cardio and strength training.
  • Maintain a calorie deficit by consuming fewer calories than you burn.
  • Get at least 7-8 hours of sleep for proper metabolism function.

What You Should Avoid:

  • Consuming excessive sugary and processed foods.
  • Skipping meals, as it can slow down metabolism.
  • Drinking too many sugary beverages like soda and juice.
  • Not getting enough sleep, as it can lead to weight gain.
  • Leading a sedentary lifestyle with little to no physical activity.
